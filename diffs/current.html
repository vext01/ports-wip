    + +Upgrading from a TeX Live package prior to 2011/05/21 will fail and requires a +manual update. + +

    +First back up anything you have manually installed in the system-wide texmf +trees (/usr/local/share/texmf*). Then you should remove any trace of TeX Live +from your system: + +

    +
    +
+pkg_delete /var/db/pkg/*texlive*

+
+
    +
